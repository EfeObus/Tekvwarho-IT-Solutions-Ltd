Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical website takes 4-8 weeks, while custom software can range from 3-6 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

How do you price your services?

We offer both project-based and retainer pricing depending on your needs. Every engagement starts with a free consultation to understand your requirements, after which we provide a transparent, detailed quote with no hidden costs.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! We love working with startups and understand the unique challenges they face. We offer flexible engagement models that work within startup budgets while still delivering high-quality solutions.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have experience across Finance, Healthcare, Education, Retail, and Technology sectors. However, our approach is adaptable to any industry—we focus on understanding your specific business challenges and delivering tailored solutions.

How do we get started?

Simply fill out the contact form above or email us directly. We'll schedule an initial meeting to understand your needs and explore how we can help. There's no obligation—just a friendly conversation to see if we're a good fit.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance and support packages for all our solutions. We believe in long-term partnerships and are committed to ensuring your technology continues to serve your business effectively.
